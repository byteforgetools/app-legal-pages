Privacy Policy

App: Image to PDF Converter
Developer: ByteForge Tools
Package name: com.byteforge.imagetopdf
Effective date: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Image to PDF Converter ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the App. The App is designed to combine images into PDF files locally on your device, without requiring an account and without uploading your photos or generated PDF files to ByteForge Tools servers.

Short version: Your selected images are processed on your device. We do not upload your images or PDF contents to our servers. The App uses Google AdMob to show ads and Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to manage consent where required by law. The App uses Google Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to collect aggregate usage statistics and crash diagnostics that help us improve stability and features.

1. Information We Process

1.1 Images and generated PDF files

The App lets you select images from your device and convert them into PDF files. The selected images are processed locally on your device. We do not upload your selected images, generated PDF files, image contents, file names, or file paths to ByteForge Tools servers.

The App may read technical information about selected images, such as dimensions, file format, file size, and orientation metadata, in order to display previews, arrange pages, rotate images, estimate processing work, and generate the PDF. This processing happens on your device.

1.2 PDF library and local app data

The App stores a local list of generated PDF files so that you can reopen, share, rename, or delete them from the "My PDFs" screen. This local library may include file names, file paths or content URIs, file sizes, page counts, and creation times. This information stays on your device and is not uploaded to ByteForge Tools servers.

Generated PDF files are saved on your device, such as under Downloads/ByteForge PDF on supported Android versions. On older Android versions, files may be saved in app-specific external storage and shared through Android's file sharing system.

1.3 App settings and backup

The App stores certain settings locally, such as theme preference, PDF default settings, PDF list sorting/view mode, rating prompt state, and basic app usage counters needed to make the App work correctly. Android system backup may back up SharedPreferences if you have device backup enabled. Generated PDF files and selected image contents are not intentionally included in the App's backup rules.

1.4 Advertising data

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect or use device identifiers, including the advertising ID, IP address, coarse location derived from IP, app interaction information, crash data, diagnostic data, and other information as described by Google's policies.

In regions where consent is required, the App uses Google's User Messaging Platform (UMP) to request and manage your choices for personalized advertising and related purposes. If you do not consent to personalized advertising, ads may still be shown, but they may be non-personalized where supported.

1.5 Feedback and support emails

If you contact us by email through the App or otherwise, we will receive the information you choose to include, such as your email address, message content, screenshots, or attachments. We use this information only to respond to your request and improve the App.

1.6 Analytics and crash reporting

The App uses Google Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand how the App is used in aggregate and to detect and fix stability problems. These tools may collect usage events (such as when PDF generation starts or completes), app and device information (such as app version, device model, operating system version, language, and approximate region derived from IP address), and crash or diagnostic data (such as stack traces and the state of the App at the time of a crash).

This information is used to improve the App and is not used by ByteForge Tools to identify you personally. We do not send your selected images, generated PDF contents, file names, or file paths to Firebase Analytics or Firebase Crashlytics.

2. Permissions

  • Photos / files selected by you: Used to let you choose images and create PDF files.
  • Internet and network state: Used by advertising and consent SDKs.
  • Advertising ID: Used by Google AdMob for advertising, subject to your consent choices and applicable law.

3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — advertising. See Google Advertising Policy.
  • Google User Messaging Platform (UMP) — consent management. See Google UMP documentation.
  • Google Play services — Android platform services used by Google SDKs. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play In-App Review — optional review prompt provided by Google Play. Your use of Google Play features is subject to Google's own terms and policies.
  • Google Firebase Analytics — aggregate usage analytics. See Firebase Privacy and Security.
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics — crash and stability diagnostics. See Firebase Privacy and Security.

4. How We Use Information

  • To provide image selection, page arrangement, PDF generation, preview, sharing, renaming, and deleting features.
  • To store and display your local PDF library.
  • To remember local settings and improve the user experience on your device.
  • To show advertisements and measure ad performance through Google AdMob.
  • To comply with consent requirements in applicable regions through Google UMP.
  • To understand aggregate usage and improve features through Google Firebase Analytics.
  • To detect, diagnose, and fix crashes and stability issues through Google Firebase Crashlytics.
  • To respond to feedback or support requests when you contact us by email.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your photos, PDF files, or personal information. We do not upload your selected images or generated PDF contents to ByteForge Tools servers. Advertising-related data, aggregate analytics data, and crash diagnostics may be processed by Google and its partners according to Google's policies and your consent choices.

When you choose to share a generated PDF, the file is shared through Android's system sharing interface with the app or person you select. You are responsible for deciding what to share and with whom.

6. Data Retention

Images you select and PDF files you create remain on your device unless you delete them. You can delete generated PDFs from within the App where available or through your device's file manager. Local app settings remain until you clear the App's data or uninstall the App. Emails you send to us may be retained as needed to respond to your request and maintain support records.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

8. Your Choices

  • You choose which images to select and process.
  • You can delete generated PDF files from the App where available or from your device storage.
  • Where available, you can manage ad consent choices through the App's "Ad preferences" option.
  • You can reset the advertising ID or limit ad personalization in your Android system settings.
  • You can clear the App's local data or uninstall the App to remove locally stored App settings.

9. Security

We use platform-provided storage and Android security controls. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure. Please be careful when selecting, saving, or sharing images and PDF files that may contain sensitive information.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
